My Favorite Movies

Here are five of my favorite movies with some details about each.

1. Interstellar

Director: Christopher Nolan

Release Year: 2014

Summary: A team of explorers travels through a wormhole in space in an attempt to ensure humanity's survival as Earth becomes uninhabitable.

2. Avengers: Infinity War

Director: Anthony and Joe Russo

Release Year: 2018

Summary: The Avengers and their allies must be willing to sacrifice all in an attempt to defeat the powerful Thanos before his blitz of devastation and ruin puts an end to the universe.

3. Avengers: Endgame

Director: Anthony and Joe Russo

Release Year: 2019

Summary: After the devastating events of Infinity War, the Avengers assemble once more to undo Thanos' actions and restore balance to the universe.

4. Inception

Director: Christopher Nolan

Release Year: 2010

Summary: A thief who enters the dreams of others to steal secrets is tasked with planting an idea into the mind of a CEO through a process called inception.

5. The Conjuring

Director: James Wan

Release Year: 2013

Summary: Paranormal investigators Ed and Lorraine Warren work to help a family terrorized by a dark presence in their farmhouse.
